Transformed society matures into its own culture. The Resistance organises in the shadows of a confident new world.

By this era, the transformed world had stopped merely surviving and begun to *flourish* on its own terms. A distinct Transformed culture emerged — art, hierarchy, ritual, and a settled way of life built around the realities of scale and power.

In the shadows of this confident new world, the first [Resistance](the-resistance) cells formed — a loose coalition of empathy-retaining women and rescued toys, working in secret to smuggle males out of the mega-cities.
