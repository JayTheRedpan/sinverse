## Overview

Project Sylph is a SinCorp initiative operating in the present day of the Sinverse timeline. It is SinCorp's most significant current undertaking. Its nature, scope, and objectives are not publicly known.

What is known — because people have noticed things, and people talk — is that SinCorp has been acquiring specific resources, personnel, and facilities at a pace and with a specificity that suggests something large is being built toward.

Whether Project Sylph is a continuation of the work that produced the Apotheosis Serum, something entirely new, or something that defies easy categorisation is a question that several people are trying very hard to answer.

## Did You Know

- Project Sylph is the only SinCorp initiative that does not appear in any externally-filed documentation. Even SinCorp's infrastructure contracts — deliberately opaque — reference other internal project codes. Project Sylph does not appear at all.
- The Resistance became aware of Project Sylph's existence not through infiltration, but because three researchers associated with SinCorp disappeared within the same month. All three had been working in the same facility.
- The name Sylph refers to a spirit of the air — ethereal, fast, and difficult to pin down. Whether Sin chose it for its meaning or its sound is unknown.
- At least one Matriarch outside Angora has quietly requested information about Project Sylph from SinCorp. She received no response. She did not ask again.
