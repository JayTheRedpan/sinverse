# Project Sylph

**Project Sylph** is a SinCorp initiative headed personally by **Sin** — and the event that marks the Sinverse's transition into a new era. Where the Apotheosis Serum remade the world in a single uncontrolled surge, Sylph is deliberate: a directed program to engineer an entirely new class of being, sitting between the towering giantesses and the toys who serve them.

## The middle class

Before Sylph, the post-TDay order was starkly two-tiered: the Transformed giantesses above, and the toys below. Project Sylph was conceived to fill the space between them — to create a **lieutenant class**, a rung of authority answerable to the giantesses and set over the toys.

The need was practical as much as structural. Many giantesses had simply **outgrown** the toys available to them; an ordinary toy could no longer satisfy a partner of immense scale. And the toy population needed managing. Sylph answered both problems at once with a single new kind of servant.

## The Sylph Serum

The project's breakthrough is a serum **modified from the Apotheosis Serum**. The original keyed off of *womanhood* — it could only ever push its subjects toward the towering feminine forms that defined the new world. Sin found a way to make the modified formula key off of **femininity in general** rather than womanhood specifically, opening transformation to subjects the original never could have taken.

The effects are **similar to the Apotheosis Serum, but weaker** — a deliberate, controlled echo rather than the full bloom:

- **Increased size**, typically growing the recipient to somewhere between **9 and 12 feet** — large enough to stand apart from the toys, but well short of the giantesses they answer to.
- **Increased organ size**, scaled to their new stature.
- **Heightened libido**, in keeping with their intended role.

The result is a Sylph: bigger, more potent, and more driven than any toy, yet unmistakably beneath the giantesses in the order of things.

## The role of a Sylph

A Sylph's purpose is **twofold**.

First, they are **satisfiers** — large and capable enough to serve the giantesses who had outgrown ordinary toys, meeting needs the smaller could no longer meet.

Second, they are **enforcers**. A Sylph keeps the toys in line, supervising and dominating them in service to the giantesses above. The closest comparison is the **butler of a grand household**: an authority over the rest of the staff, yet one whose every action is ultimately in service to the mistress he answers to. A Sylph rules the toys precisely because he so thoroughly belongs to the giantesses.

## Selection

Sin selects Sylph candidates personally, and only toys meeting **three criteria** are chosen:

1. **They embrace femininity.** Candidates are drawn from those who already lean into it — femboys and the like — since the modified serum keys off femininity itself.
2. **They embrace the new world order.** A Sylph must be genuinely accepting of the giantesses' rule. The role is one of devoted service, not coerced labor, and it cannot be filled by the resentful.
3. **They prove the role.** After the serum's initial application, the candidate must — willingly — dominate a toy in front of the giantesses, demonstrating before witnesses that they accept and embody their new place: above the toys, beneath the giantesses.

Only once all three are satisfied is a candidate fully a Sylph. The final test is exactly that — a test. What Sin grants, Sin can withdraw just as easily, and a candidate who fails the third criterion has her blessing removed as readily as it was given. Those who fail are never heard from again.

## Significance

If the Apotheosis Serum was the event that ended the old world, Project Sylph is the one reshaping what came after — taking the rigid two-tier order TDay left behind and giving it a middle, a hierarchy, a chain of command. It is Sin's signature work of the present era, and the initiative driving the Sinverse forward into it.

## Did You Know?

- Project Sylph is headed **personally by Sin**, and its launch marks the boundary into a new era of the timeline.
- The Sylph Serum's key innovation is keying off of **femininity** rather than **womanhood** — the one barrier the original Apotheosis Serum could never cross.
- Sylphs typically stand **9–12 feet** tall: above the toys, but far beneath the giantesses they serve.
- The name evokes the **sylph**, an air elemental — a lighter, refined counterpart to the overwhelming force of the original serum.
