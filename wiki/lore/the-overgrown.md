## Overview

**The Overgrown** are the monstrous living things of the [Wilds](the-wilds) — the enormous, and often predatory, flora and fauna that grew out of the world [TDay](tday) unmade. After TDay, nature grew and expanded to reclaim the old world, forming the Wilds — and the Overgrown grew with it.

The name is a collective one, and deliberately broad. It covers the building-sized beasts and the predatory growths alike, because in the Wilds the distinction rarely matters to the thing being eaten. To a toy, "the Overgrown" is not a category so much as a fact: everything out here is too big, most of it is hungry, and very little of it can be reasoned with.

## What they are

The Overgrown are a byproduct of the [TDay Variant](apotheosis-serum) — the unstable formulation of the serum that saturated not just the women it [transformed](the-transformed) but the living world itself, through water, air, and soil. Where it touched the wilderness, nature answered the only way it could: it grew, and it grew wrong.

This is the crucial distinction between the Overgrown and the [Ferals](ferals), with whom they are sometimes carelessly lumped together. A Feral was once a *person* — a Transformed woman whose mind the serum destroyed. An Overgrown thing was never a mind at all. It is an animal or a plant that the Variant swelled to impossible scale, sometimes into outright predation, but never into thought. One is a tragedy; the other is simply a hazard of the country.

SinCorp's own literature files them clinically as **Variant megaflora and megafauna**. Everyone below the giantesses just calls them the Overgrown.

The Variant left its other marks on them as well. Chief among these is the same heightened libido the serum drives into everything it touches — and in a beast or a growth with no mind to govern it, that drive answers to instinct alone. The consequence for a toy is a grim one: predation in the Wilds is not always simply lethal. Some of the Overgrown, the predatory flora included, subject their prey to sexual assault as readily as they consume it. It is recorded here as a plain fact of the country rather than dwelt upon — one more of the Wilds' indifferent cruelties, an appetite with no malice or awareness behind it and no less brutal for the lack.

## The Overgrown Flora

The flora is the quieter horror, and to many the worse one. Plants swelled to enormous size across the Wilds, and a portion of them crossed a line no plant should — turning predatory, learning to feed on the warm-blooded things that move among them.

A predatory growth does not hunt the way a beast does. It waits. It seizes a toy in its vines or its maw and feeds at leisure — and the manner of it is its own particular cruelty: such a growth will violate a toy as readily as it consumes him, a slow death with no malice behind it, no intent to appeal to, and no one to hear the appeal. There is nothing to outwit and nothing to reason with, only a thing that has grown large enough and hungry enough to make a meal of a man, and all the patience in the world.

Whole stretches of the Wilds lie in permanent twilight beneath canopies grown so dense the sun never reaches the floor — and it is in exactly those green-dark places that the predatory flora does best.

## The Overgrown Fauna

The fauna is the louder horror. Animals grew to enormous and sometimes monstrous scale, and at that size even gentleness turns lethal.

The Wilds are full of deaths delivered without intent by things simply too large to notice them. It is not unusual for a toy to be devoured by a doe the size of a building — a placid herbivore in every respect but the one that matters, cropping vegetation and swallowing whatever small thing happens to be among it, never registering that it has killed. The truly predatory Overgrown fauna are worse in the obvious way; but it is the harmless-shaped ones, the ones that would flee a toy at their old size, that kill most casually. Size alone is the whole of the danger.

## The Loosed Experiments

Not everything in the Overgrown grew there by accident. Every [mega-city](mega-cities)'s [SinCorp](sincorp) branch runs its own undisclosed research, and when a laboratory tires of a creation — an experiment grown inconvenient, unstable, or simply boring — the simplest disposal is to turn it loose in the Wilds and let the country swallow it.

Whatever those things become, they become it out there, uncatalogued and answerable to no one, adding new horrors to a place that needed none. They are counted among the Overgrown by default, because there is no one out there to sort them into any other category — and because, to a toy, a loosed SinCorp horror and a monstrous natural one arrive at the same ending.

## The Overgrown and the toy

For a [Wild Toy](toys), the Overgrown are the constant, ambient condition of the Wilds — not an event but the weather. They are the reason the Wilds are, by every practical measure, far more dangerous than any mega-city, and they contend for a toy's life alongside the [macro Transformed](the-wilds), the Ferals, and the [Paws](the-paws) who come hunting.

But the Overgrown are also, strangely, part of the bargain that makes the Wilds worth it. A city offers a toy a managed cruelty with an owner's name on it; the Wilds offer an *un*managed one, with no name on it at all. The Overgrown do not own a toy, register him, or care what he is. They will simply eat him if they can — and for some, that indifference is closer to freedom than anything the cities allow.

## Did You Know?

- The Overgrown and the [Ferals](ferals) are two entirely different things often confused: a Feral was a *person* whose mind the serum broke; an Overgrown thing was never a mind at all, just an animal or plant the [TDay Variant](apotheosis-serum) swelled to monstrous scale.
- The term covers **both flora and fauna** on purpose — in the Wilds, a predatory tree and a building-sized beast pose the same kind of threat, so they carry the same collective name.
- The most casual killers in the Wilds are often the *harmless-shaped* Overgrown — a giant herbivore that swallows a toy without ever noticing, rather than a predator that means to.
- SinCorp's own disposals become part of the Overgrown: unwanted lab experiments are released into the Wilds, where they join the category by default.
- Like everything the serum touched, the Overgrown carry its **heightened libido** — which is why predation in the Wilds is sometimes sexual as well as lethal, the predatory flora included.
- SinCorp's clinical name for them is **Variant megaflora and megafauna** — but no one outside a report calls them anything but the Overgrown.
