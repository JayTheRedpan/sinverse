## Overview

SinCorp is the organization founded by Sin in the years leading up to TDay, built from the shell of the bio-research laboratory where she first developed the Apotheosis Serum. Today it operates across every major mega-city as the closest thing the post-TDay world has to a global institution.

Its stated purpose is biological research and urban services. Its actual purpose is whatever Sin decides it is.

## Structure

SinCorp operates through a network of regional divisions, each nominally reporting to a central administrative body in Angora. In practice, the divisions operate with significant autonomy — regional directors interpret SinCorp's broad directives and execute them as they see fit, as long as results are delivered.

Internal hierarchy is meritocratic in the narrow sense that SinCorp rewards capability without sentiment. Promotion is fast for those who produce. The consequences of failure are not.

## Activities

SinCorp's visible activities include:

- **Urban infrastructure** — SinCorp holds contracts for water, power, and logistics in most mega-cities
- **Pharmaceutical production** — including the ongoing production and controlled distribution of Apotheosis Serum derivatives
- **Research** — biological, chemical, and experimental; the nature of ongoing research is not publicly disclosed

Its less visible activities are the source of most stories in the Sinverse universe. SinCorp experiments are frequently the catalyst for new events, characters, and complications.

## Relationship to the Mega-Cities

Each mega-city Matriarch maintains nominal independence from SinCorp. In practice, SinCorp's infrastructure contracts create dependencies that give it significant leverage. Sin does not use this leverage openly. She does not need to.

## Did You Know

- SinCorp was founded three years before TDay, operating publicly as a mid-tier pharmaceutical research company. No one who worked there at that time has publicly discussed what they actually knew.
- SinCorp controls the water supply of every major mega-city. This is not a coincidence.
- The company has no public-facing leadership other than Sin herself. There are no press releases, no earnings calls, no external board. It simply operates, and the world accommodates it.
- SinCorp employs more Transformed researchers than any other organisation in the world. It also employs a significant number of toys in administrative and service roles. They are not permitted in research facilities.
- At least three Resistance operations have been mounted against SinCorp facilities. None have resulted in public disclosure of what those facilities contained.
