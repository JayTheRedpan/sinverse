## Grazers

*Overgrown — Fauna*

**Grazers** are the great herbivores of the [Wilds](the-wilds): deer, cattle, and their like, swelled by the [TDay Variant](apotheosis-serum) to the size of buildings. They are the most numerous of the large [Overgrown](the-overgrown), and — counterintuitively — among the deadliest things a [toy](toys) can meet out there.

## What makes them dangerous

Nothing about a Grazer's intent. A Grazer is exactly as placid and harmless as the animal it once was; it wants only to crop the enormous vegetation of the Wilds and move on. The danger is scale alone. A toy caught in the wrong place is simply swept up and swallowed with a mouthful of foliage, or crushed underfoot, by an animal that never registers he was there at all. It is not unusual to see a toy killed by a doe the size of a tower — a death delivered without malice, notice, or any possibility of appeal.

Like all the [Overgrown](the-overgrown), a Grazer carries the serum's heightened libido — even an animal far too vast and too placid to register anything as small as a toy, it may still turn that appetite on one. A Grazer's danger stays what it always was: hunger, both for food and outlets.

For a [Wild Toy](wild-toys), this makes Grazers a peculiar terror: there is no predator's logic to outwit, no hunger to wait out. There is only the need to never, ever be underfoot.
