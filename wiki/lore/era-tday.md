Transformation Day. The serum is released worldwide. The single moment that divides all of Sinverse history.

On [TDay](tday), [Sin](sin) triggered the planned cataclysm. The [Apotheosis Serum](apotheosis-serum) was released into the world simultaneously through water supplies, airborne dispersal, and SinCorp's global distribution network. Women across the world began transforming — growing, changing, and in most cases losing their empathy. The old world collapsed within weeks.

TDay is not a span of time but a single pivotal moment. Every other date in the Sinverse is measured relative to it: *T-N* for years before, *T+N* for years after.
