## Snarebloom

*Overgrown — Flora*

**Snarebloom** is a common name for the predatory canopy growths of the [Wilds](the-wilds) — flowering vines and blooms, swelled by the [TDay Variant](apotheosis-serum), that turned carnivorous somewhere in the growing. They favour the permanent twilight beneath the Wilds' dense canopy, where they hang patient and near-invisible until something warm moves within reach.

## What makes them dangerous

A Snarebloom does not hunt so much as *wait*. It seizes what comes near — a [toy](toys) most readily of all — winding its vines tight around its catch to grab and restrict it, holding the prey fast while it feeds at leisure: a slow death with nothing behind it to reason with and no one to hear the appeal. Because it does not move or chase, it is easy to miss until it has already closed, which is much of what makes the green-dark stretches of the Wilds so feared.

A feeding Snarebloom is seldom quiet — or rather, its prey is not. The cries of a caught toy as it is violated carry far through the still, dense air beneath the canopy, and the plant has, in its blind way, come to rely on them: the sound draws the curious and the hungry alike toward its source, where the waiting vines take whatever wanders within reach. A single catch, in this way, often becomes several — each new arrival lured in by the last, until a well-placed Snarebloom sits at the quiet centre of a small graveyard.

Like all the [Overgrown](the-overgrown), a Snarebloom carries the serum's heightened libido with no mind to govern it, and an unlucky toy may be subjected to that through its vines as readily as to being consumed — one more of the plant's indifferent, instinct-driven cruelties.
