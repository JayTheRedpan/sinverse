Sin develops the serum and founds [SinCorp](sincorp) in secret. The old world's final years, unaware of what is coming.

During this era, Sin — then a research scientist — synthesised the first successful batch of the [Apotheosis Serum](apotheosis-serum) and administered it to herself, becoming the first of the Transformed. Working in the shadows, she manoeuvred herself into control of her research lab, rebranded it as SinCorp, and began quietly mass-producing the serum while planning its global release.

To the rest of the world, these were unremarkable years. Beneath the surface, the instrument of its transformation was being built at industrial scale.
