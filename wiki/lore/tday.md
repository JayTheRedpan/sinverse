## Overview

TDay — Transformation Day — is the singular event that divides all of Sinverse history. The day Sin's Apotheosis Serum was released upon the world simultaneously and without warning. Every date in the Sinverse universe is measured relative to this moment, designated **T±0**. Events before TDay are written as **T-N** (N years prior); events after as **T+N** (N years following).

## What Happened

In the weeks prior to TDay, SinCorp had quietly positioned itself across global supply chains, water treatment infrastructure, and distribution networks. When Sin gave the order, the serum was released through every channel simultaneously — airborne dispersal, contaminated water supplies, and direct delivery through SinCorp's commercial products.

Within hours, women across the world began to change.

The transformation was not subtle. Bodies swelled and grew. In most cases the process took minutes. Clothing tore. Buildings collapsed as newly minted giantesses stumbled through streets built for a smaller world. The empathy suppressant took hold alongside the growth, and many of the Transformed — disoriented, aroused, and no longer particularly concerned with the wellbeing of those around them — acted accordingly.

## The Chaos Period

The weeks following TDay are sometimes referred to simply as **The Chaos**. Civil infrastructure collapsed in most major population centres. Male casualties were significant. Some women who had not yet been exposed fled, though there was no escaping a serum dispersed through the atmosphere.

The Chaos lasted approximately six weeks before the dominant Transformed began asserting control over territories, and the shape of the new world started to emerge.

## Legacy

TDay is the anchor of the Sinverse timeline. It is T±0 — the moment everything changed. For the Transformed, it is often spoken of with reverence, even celebration. For toys and Resistance members, it is a wound that never fully closes.

*T±0 is not simply a date. It is the border between two entirely different worlds.*
