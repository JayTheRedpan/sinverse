## Overview

A quick-reference index to the key terms, classes, places, and powers of the Sinverse. Each entry is a one-line gloss with a link to its full page — a map of the world for anyone finding their bearings, and a fast way to jump wherever your curiosity points. For individual people, see the **Characters** section; this page is for the concepts.

## The transformation

- **[The Apotheosis Serum](apotheosis-serum)** — the agent that remade the world: keys off womanhood, grows a woman into a giantess, and rewrites her empathy.
- **[TDay](tday)** — the day [Sin](sin) released the serum across the whole world at once; the dividing line of all history, and the point every date is measured from.
- **The TDay Variant** — the unstable formulation released on [TDay](tday); responsible for the [macro](macro) outliers and for overgrowing the [Wilds](the-wilds). *(See [the serum's versions](apotheosis-serum).)*
- **Empathy drop** — the colloquial name for what the [serum](apotheosis-serum) does to a [Transformed](the-transformed) woman's mind: empathy toward males severed, empathy toward women intensified. It removes a *brake* rather than installing a hunger for cruelty, and rewards the exercise of **dominion in any form** — ownership, command, adoration, fear, use, or cruelty alike.
- **Asymmetric empathy** — [SinCorp's](sincorp) clinical term for the empathy drop.
- **Feralization** — the rare failure in which the [serum](apotheosis-serum) destroys a mind rather than remaking it *(see [Ferals](ferals))*.

## Classes of being

- **[The Transformed](the-transformed)** — "giantesses"; the ruling class of towering women the serum created, near-ageless and wired to stand at the top of the order.
- **[The Tempered](tempered)** — the rare [Transformed](the-transformed) who kept their empathy; giantesses in body, outsiders in conscience, and the moral core of the [Resistance](the-resistance).
- **[Ferals](ferals)** — [Transformed](the-transformed) whose minds the serum destroyed outright, left to instinct; a creation outlier, usually ordinary-sized, that haunts the [Wilds](the-wilds).
- **[Macro](macro)** — [Transformed](the-transformed) grown far past the norm (often 100+ feet), too vast for the cities and dramatically altered in mind; each rules her own stretch of the [Wilds](the-wilds).
- **[Sylph](project-sylph)** — the engineered lieutenant class set between the giantesses and the toys; satisfiers and enforcers, chosen personally by [Sin](sin).
- **[Toys](toys)** — the world's term for males: not citizens but property, with no rights of their own.
- **[Wild Toys](wild-toys)** — [toys](toys) who live free in the [Wilds](the-wilds), uncaught or escaped, surviving outside the cities' rule.
- **[Matriarchs](matriarchs)** — the giantesses who rule the [mega-cities](mega-cities), each absolute within her own walls; above them all stands [Sin](sin), the **First Matriarch**.

## Organizations

- **[SinCorp](sincorp)** — the organisation [Sin](sin) built around herself: the instrument of [TDay](tday), and now the closest thing to a global institution.
- **[R-Labs](r-labs)** — the [SinCorp](sincorp) division that handles bulk and substandard [toys](toys) — a threat and an ending, to a toy.
- **[The Paws](the-paws)** — the Huntresses; a leaderless sisterhood of giantesses who range into the [Wilds](the-wilds) to capture [Wild Toys](wild-toys) for the cities' trade.
- **[The Resistance](the-resistance)** — a leaderless network of cells that smuggles [toys](toys) out of the cities toward [Bastion](bastion).

## Places

- **[Angora](angora)** — the first and most powerful [mega-city](mega-cities); [Sin's](sin) seat, [SinCorp's](sincorp) global headquarters, and the unofficial capital.
- **[Alea Panthera](alea-panthera)** — a [mega-city](mega-cities) known for consuming [toys](toys) faster than any other.
- **[Marisol](marisol)** — a [mega-city](mega-cities) of the new world.
- **[The mega-cities](mega-cities)** — the ordered island city-states amid the [Wilds](the-wilds), each an independent nation under a [Matriarch](matriarchs).
- **[The Wilds](the-wilds)** — nearly the whole planet: the overgrown, monstrous country that reclaimed the old world, and the only place a [toy](toys) can be free.
- **[Bastion](bastion)** — the rumoured (and real) lasting settlement of [Wild Toys](wild-toys), protected above all by no one being certain it exists.

## The bestiary

- **[The Overgrown](the-overgrown)** — the monstrous flora and fauna of the [Wilds](the-wilds), swelled by the [TDay Variant](apotheosis-serum) into things too large and often too predatory to survive among.
- **[Grazers](grazers)** — building-sized [Overgrown](the-overgrown) herbivores that kill [toys](toys) by sheer oblivious scale.
- **[Snarebloom](snarebloom)** — predatory [Overgrown](the-overgrown) flora that snares and feeds, and lures more prey with the cries of what it has caught.

## Society & the toy trade

- **[Toy Modifications](toy-modifications)** — [SinCorp's](sincorp) catalogue of alterations that make [toys](toys) survivable, useful, and reproducible.
- **[Reproduction & the Population](reproduction)** — how the [toy](toys) population, once collapsing, was stabilized and is now kept steady against bottomless demand.
- **[Life of a Toy](life-of-a-toy)** — the new world seen from the bottom.
- **[Life of a Giantess](life-of-a-giantess)** — the same world seen from the top.

## The central figure

- **[Sin](sin)** — a black bunny and research scientist; creator of the [Apotheosis Serum](apotheosis-serum), architect of [TDay](tday), head of [SinCorp](sincorp), ruler of [Angora](angora), and the First [Matriarch](matriarchs) of the world she made.
