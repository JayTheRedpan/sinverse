## Overview

Haven is not someone you want to cross. A true psychopath lacking in empathy entirely - not just for toys - she has no issue doing what it takes to achieve her own goals. However rather than seeking to climb the social ladder she prefers to occupy a position in society where she can take advantage of other people's toys without consequence.

## Appearance

Haven is a bobcat. Feline in form with a short stubby tail, long fur on her cheeks, large footpaws, and ears accentuated with tufts of fur on the tips. The fur on her chest and limbs is predominantly white with purple fur on her most of her head, back, shoulders, and thighs. She has a mix of white and dark purple spots on her back and shoulders. Purple stripes akin to natural bracelets and anklets are on her wrists and ankles. Her build is athletic - noticable musculature on her arms and legs make it clear that she posesses significant physical strength even by the new standards of the world. What might be most striking about her appearance is her eyes. Black sclera with bright yellow pupils it is impossible to ignore her peircing gaze.

## Personality

Effortlessly confident, self-assured, calm, and domineering - Haven is a psychopath through and through. She already lacked empathy prior to T-Day and the trait carried forwards after her transformation. She is primarily interested in serving herself and indulging her impulses. Though she sometimes feels an urge to prey upon her fellow giantesses she never acts upon it as toys are abundant and can be used and abused mostly without consequence making them a much better outlet for her urges. 

## History

### Before TDay

A sadistic psychopath, prior to T-Day she was a serial killer who primarily targeted men. She usually seduced them, before having sex and killing them. On T-Day she was actually about to bed and kill some guy she met online. Following exposure to the aerosolized serum she loomed over him and realized she could streamline her two-step process into one single extremely enjoyable step for her.

### After TDay

Haven now works for a maintenance company leading a crew of labour toys who help maintain the megacity she resides in. Toys under her watch frequently suffer fatal damage but managment turns a blind eye as long as the work gets done. It's in this line of work where she crossed paths with her cousin Turbo and claimed him for herself.
