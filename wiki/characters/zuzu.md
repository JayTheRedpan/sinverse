## Overview

Zuzu is one of the Transformed who did not come through the change intact. A wolf who grew past the macro threshold, she emerged on the far side of it with her mind all but gone — a towering, animalistic thing that moves on instinct alone. Among the women of SinCorp she is spoken of less as a person than as one of the "ferals": dangerous, unpredictable Transformed who have lost whatever self they once had, and whom even Sin's enforcers give a wide berth. Her ground lies in the wild zones beyond the walls — territory marked off-limits precisely because of what roams it.

## Appearance

Zuzu is a wolf with muddy brown fur over most of her body, fading to a lighter underbelly. Her hands end in claws that are strikingly large even for a Transformed of her scale. She has heterochromatic eyes — one a reddish brown, the other green — set in a face that rarely carries any human expression. A short crop of deep red hair sits atop her head, vivid and incongruous against the dull brown of her coat. She is most often seen hunched forward onto all fours, carrying herself like a beast rather than a woman, with the low, prowling gait of something that has forgotten how to stand as a person.

## Personality

There is, in any meaningful sense, very little personality left to describe. The transformation that remade Zuzu also hollowed her out: higher thought, language, and self-awareness are essentially gone, leaving raw instinct in their place. She does not scheme, speak, or play the cruel social games the other Transformed delight in — she reacts, to movement and scent and the presence of prey, and little else. What remains is closer to a wild animal than a woman: territorial, reactive, and without the human cognition that might let her be reasoned with or predicted.

### After TDay

Zuzu's transformation carried her past the macro threshold — the point beyond which the serum's effect on the mind turns catastrophic rather than merely cruel. Where most Transformed keep their wits, those who grow large enough suffer a steep cognitive collapse, and Zuzu collapsed almost entirely, emerging as one of the feral Transformed rather than a functioning part of Sin's order. She now lives apart from SinCorp society, roaming the off-limits wild zones. SinCorp personnel treat any encounter with her or the other ferals as a serious hazard; she is referenced as having left at least one captive mind-destroyed, and the ferals as a group are considered too dangerous to engage.

## Usage Guide

Neutral notes for writing her consistently:

- **She is non-verbal.** Zuzu has no dialogue. She doesn't banter, taunt, or negotiate the way other Transformed do — she acts, she doesn't talk.
- **She is feral, not clever-cruel.** Don't write her plotting or relishing cruelty as a personality trait. She runs on instinct, closer to a force of nature or an apex animal than a character with intent.
- **She is an environmental threat.** She works best as a hazard of the wild zones — something other characters fear, avoid, or blunder into — rather than someone with her own social arc.

## Did You Know

- SinCorp guards refer to Zuzu and her kind only as "the ferals," and consider the zones they inhabit too dangerous to patrol.
