## Overview

Ira is a blue jay and one of the apotheosis girls who turned the first day of the new world into a reckoning. Before TDay she was a receptionist at a high-rise investment firm — hired, by her own bitter understanding, to be decoration: a pretty face at the front desk for a floor full of swaggering salesmen to leer at. She spent a decade swallowing their harassment because the alternative was losing the job. When the serum took her, it took her mid-assault, and whatever restraint a lifetime of biting her tongue had built in her did not survive the transformation.

She stands nearly sixty feet tall now, a towering, jet-taloned predator with a prehensile endowment, and she hunts. The men who built their careers on treating women like furniture were exactly the kind she came up under, and on TDay she stopped being the one who had to take it.

## Appearance

Ira is a blue jay rendered on a catastrophic scale — black, white, and brilliant blue plumage; powerful feathered wings; and the long jet-black talons of a bird of prey, here sharpened to a size that splits pavement and sinks into concrete like wet sand. Her eyes are a striking turquoise, and on her old face they were the prettiest thing about a person the firm reduced to scenery. On her new one they read very differently: a spotlight of a stare that pins whatever it lands on.

The serum left her endowed in place of the genitalia it took — in her case a tentacle-like, prehensile member rather than the equine or canine forms many of her peers carry. At rest it retracts fully into a cloaca-like slit, leaving her smooth and featureless until she chooses otherwise; she has no external testicles. It is as much a tool of the hunt as anything else she has, and she uses it like one.

## Personality

Before, Ira was the model of swallowed resentment — outwardly composed, professionally pleasant, privately seething. She knew exactly what she was to the men around her and exactly why complaining would cost her, so she didn't. That long-practiced restraint was the load-bearing wall of her personality, and TDay knocked it flat.

What's left is wrath with nothing holding it back. Ira is sardonic, theatrical, and entirely unbothered by what she's become — she banters with her prey, makes games of their last moments, and takes open pleasure in the inversion of power that the new world handed her. Once she had to perform politeness for men who saw her as an object; now she performs nothing, and the men who cross her path are the ones who have to beg. She does not agonize over the line she's crossed. As far as Ira is concerned, the line was crossed against her first, for years, and she is simply done being on the wrong side of it.

## History

### Before TDay

Ira worked the front desk at a Wall Street-style investment firm run by a sneering millionaire who staffed his sales floor almost exclusively with big cats — a preference he justified with ugly talk about "predatory instinct" — and who hired Ira to sit out front as eye candy. Her dress code was dictated to "accommodate her figure"; theirs was sharp suits. Every morning she absorbed an hour of pickup lines, gawking, and casual contempt from men who were never disciplined for it, because the boss considered her discomfort part of the décor.

The one exception was **the intern** — a young, green, decent newcomer, and the only person on the floor who bothered to learn her name and treat her like one. Small kindnesses from him, a coffee and a kind word, were the thing that made the place survivable.

### TDay

When the transformations began rolling across the globe, Ira watched the early reports from her desk, half-amused, assuming a hoax. She didn't get to finish the thought. One of the salesmen, emboldened and unsupervised over the lunch hour, dragged her into the alley beside the building to assault her. The intern — hearing her — threw himself into the attacker to buy her time, and took a brutal beating for it.

It was there, cornered and terrified and watching the only good person at that job get pummeled on her behalf, that the apotheosis serum ignited in Ira. The change came fast and overwhelming: heat, weight, and then growth, the alley and the office and the men in it shrinking away beneath her until she sat nude across a four-lane street, taller than the building she'd worked in. The two men who'd been fighting over her froze. The balance of the entire world had inverted in the span of a panic attack, and Ira — for the first time in a decade — held all of it.

*What became of the intern in the chaos that followed is unrecorded.*

### After TDay

Ira did not wait to find out whether her new power was permanent. Treating it as a gift that might be revoked at any moment, she threw herself into the new world with abandon — and what she chose to do with it was hunt. The "alpha-male" types who had spent her whole working life treating her as prey became her prey, and from there the appetite generalized. Whatever justification the first day carried, Ira is now an indiscriminate predator, stalking the mega-cities for men with the same casual entitlement she once endured, and disposing of them with the same indifference they once showed her.

She is one of the apotheosis girls who never sought structure or allegiance afterward — no faction, no cause beyond her own satisfaction. The serum gave her size, strength, and a bottomless appetite, and removed the lifetime of restraint that had kept all three in check.

## As a Predator

Ira is a hunter in the most literal sense the new world allows. She works the streets and alleys of the mega-cities, using her size to corner prey and her prehensile endowment to seize and toy with them before the end. That appendage is the centerpiece of her technique: she uses it like a second limb to grab toys outright — sometimes simply crushing them in its grip — and, more insidiously, to reach where her hands and bulk cannot. A toy that has wedged itself into a drainpipe, a collapsed doorway, or a gap between buildings, thinking itself safely out of reach of something so enormous, will instead watch that pink, questing length come worming in after it. The reveal of the appendage is its own kind of terror: a toy that has been keeping just out of her grasp suddenly understands that her reach is far longer and far stranger than they assumed, and that there was never really anywhere to hide. She is fond of cruel little games — bargains her quarry cannot win, taunts dressed up as mercy — and she draws open, theatrical pleasure from the terror of men who, not long ago, would have been the ones doing the cornering. There is no negotiating with her and no appealing to a better nature; the part of her that performed patience for men is the part that died on TDay.

## Preferences

*The kinks and dynamics that define this character's encounters.*

Ira's signature is the **constriction** she practices with her prehensile endowment. She uses the appendage like a boa — coiling it around a toy's body and squeezing, savoring the helpless struggle and the slow tightening of her grip far more than she savors any conventional act. The constricting hold is where her pleasure lives: the feeling of a captured toy writhing and straining against coils they cannot hope to overpower, the steady increase of pressure entirely at her whim. She draws it out, alternating between crushing and easing off to keep her prey aware and afraid — but the defining detail of Ira's dynamic is *when* she chooses to finish. For her, the climax and the crush are one and the same moment: as her pleasure crests, the coils clench in helpless reflex, and the final, bone-deep squeeze that ends a toy lands at the exact peak of her own release. She has learned to ride a toy's struggle right to that edge and let go of her restraint at the precise instant the pressure becomes fatal, so that the last thing a toy ever feels is the appendage convulsing tight around them as she rides out her orgasm. The reveal of the appendage and the first wrap of those coils is, for the toy, where the terror truly begins; for Ira, the toy's final moment and her own are inseparable.

## Usage Guide

*Notes for content creators working with this character.*

- Ira's defining engine is **inverted power** — she was prey for a decade and is now the apex. Her cruelty reads as wrath unchained, not motiveless sadism; keep the throughline of "she was on the receiving end first."
- She is an **indiscriminate predator** post-TDay, not a discerning avenger — don't write her as carefully targeting only the guilty.
- **The intern** (unnamed) is her one pre-TDay soft spot and his fate is deliberately **unwritten** — leave it open unless coordinated.
- She has **no faction or allegiance** — she answers to her own appetite.
