## Overview

Jay is a red panda and a toy — one of the males who survived TDay and built a life in the spaces the new world left for people like him. He is the progenitor of the Sinverse universe: the character through whom its stories are told, its lore documented, and its world explored.

He is 5'7". In the post-TDay world, that is not a remarkable height for a toy. It is remarkable for how little it has limited him.

## Appearance

Jay is a compact, athletic red panda with the characteristic rust-and-cream colouration of his species. His most distinctive feature is his chest crest — a personal emblem he wears as a mark of identity.

## Personality

Jay is curious, adaptable, and possessed of a dry humour that has served him better than most survival skills. He is not reckless, but he is not timid either. He occupies the space between those things with a kind of practised ease that suggests he has had to think carefully about which risks are worth taking.

He documents things. The Sinverse wiki exists, in part, because Jay finds it important that someone does.

## Lore / History

### Before TDay

Jay's life before TDay is documented privately rather than publicly. What is known is that he was alive and adult before TDay, which means he lived through a world that no longer exists and carries the memory of it.

### After TDay

Jay survived the Chaos. The specifics of how are part of his story, which is still being written. He ended up in the Wilds — not by accident, and not without cost.

## Did You Know

- Jay chose his crest emblem himself. It predates TDay. He considers it non-negotiable.
- He is one of the few toys who writes things down — not just for himself, but with the understanding that someone else might need to read it later.
- Jay has a complicated relationship with the word "toy." He uses it. He is not comfortable with it. He has decided that discomfort is the honest response.
- He is 5'7" tall. In the old world, that was average. In the new world, it is the tallest a toy can be and still be considered unremarkable by the Transformed. Jay is aware of this.
- The Sinverse wiki was Jay's idea. He has not explained his reasoning publicly, but those who know him suggest it has something to do with the belief that a world no one documents is a world that can be lied about freely.
