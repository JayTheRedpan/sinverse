## Overview

Anna is a spotted hyena giantess, transformed by the [Apotheosis Serum](apotheosis-serum) on [TDay](tday), and a creature of appetite and impulse. She is best understood as an opportunist: a giantess who acts on her whims the moment they strike her, with little patience and less restraint, and who is entirely willing to help herself to a toy that isn't hers if the mood takes her. What defines her in the encounter is scent and fear — Anna dominates through her own overwhelming musk and revels in the terror it provokes in something small and helpless beneath her.

Much about Anna remains undefined. She holds no known title or allegiance, and little of her history has been established beyond the obvious fact of her transformation. What is clear is the shape of her cruelty: casual, sensory, and gleeful, the cruelty of a giantess who treats the small as toys to be picked up and played with on a whim.

## Appearance

Anna is a tall, powerfully built spotted hyena, standing around seventeen feet. Her frame is robust and muscular — well-defined abs, arms strong enough to handle a toy like a doll — covered in tan fur marked with a hyena's spotted pattern. Her eyes are a striking red, and she wears her long black hair half over her face, often framing a wide, toothy grin.

Two details set her apart. A pair of devil-style horns rise from her head — an unusual and unexplained feature even by the standards of [the transformed](the-transformed). And her nipples are each pierced with a bar, set against the dark skin beneath her fur. Like her hyena kin she carries equine anatomy, a product of her transformation.

## Personality

Anna is impulsive, indulgent, and ruled by her appetites. She is the kind of giantess who sees something she wants and simply takes it, untroubled by whether it belongs to her, whether the moment is convenient, or whether anyone might object. When a whim strikes — often loosened further by drink — she follows it then and there, sweeping up a toy and carrying it off to amuse herself with no more thought than a child grabbing a plaything.

Her cruelty is playful rather than calculated. Where some giantesses are cold and methodical, Anna is gleeful and sensory, delighting in the immediate physical experience of domination and in the obvious distress of the small thing at her mercy. She grins through it, narrates her own enjoyment, and treats the whole affair as her fun — the toy's terror being, if anything, part of the appeal rather than an obstacle to it.

There is a careless, even reckless quality to her. She doesn't fuss over ownership or consequences, and her treatment of toys is rough and heedless of their fragility. She is, in short, a giantess who takes what she wants when she wants it, and enjoys herself thoroughly while she does — a being of impulse and appetite with the size to indulge both freely.

## History

### Before TDay

Little is known of who Anna was before the world changed. Like all the transformed, she lived an ordinary life before the Apotheosis Serum remade her — but the specifics have not been established.

### After TDay

Anna was transformed by the Apotheosis Serum on TDay, growing into the towering hyena giantess she is now and taking her place among the dominant women of the new world. Beyond that, her story remains largely unwritten: she holds no recorded title, faction, or role, and moves through the new world as an opportunist rather than a figure of any established power. What encounters she leaves behind are marked by the same signature — a toy swept up on a whim, overwhelmed by her scent, and left shaken by her careless, gleeful cruelty.
