## Overview

Hannah is a kangaroo giantess and the [Matriarch](matriarchs) of [Marisol](marisol), the new world's sun-soaked island pleasure-capital. Where her fellow Matriarchs rule through cold order or underworld cunning, Hannah rules through sheer exuberant magnetism — she is the life of the endless party, the most vivid presence on an island built for revelry, and she holds her city by being the gravitational centre of its perpetual celebration.

Boundless, energetic, and impossible to ignore, Hannah governs Marisol the way she does everything: at full tilt, with a grin, and with the easy confidence of a giantess who has never met a good time she couldn't lead. Beneath the celebration she is a Matriarch like any other — but her power wears a sunhat and a drink, and feels less like a throne than the centre of the dance floor.

## Appearance

Hannah is a kangaroo giantess of considerable size — standing around thirty-three feet, she towers well above the more modest giantesses, a tall, athletic, powerfully-built figure suited to the boundless energy she's known for. Her frame is lean and strong, all spring and motion, and she carries herself with the loose, restless vitality of someone perpetually half a beat into the next thing.

She dresses for the island and the eternal summer — bright, casual, sun-ready — and wears her gold-blonde hair in a long braid, with bright green eyes that match her irrepressible energy. Her transformation left her with equine anatomy, towering over the toys who serve Marisol's leisure. More than any single feature, what people remember about Hannah is the energy: she is rarely still, rarely quiet, and never far from the centre of whatever's happening.

## Personality

Hannah is exuberant, magnetic, and boundlessly energetic — a giantess who lives at full volume and draws everyone around her into her orbit. She is the life of the party in the most literal sense: the one who starts the dancing, leads the revelry, and turns any gathering into an event simply by being in it. That magnetism is the engine of her rule. Marisol is a city built for pleasure, and Hannah governs it by being the most pleasurable presence on the island — the centre everyone else naturally arranges themselves around.

Her warmth is real, and so is the lightness of her touch compared to her fellow Matriarchs. She is not a schemer like Alea's ruler or a figure of severe order like Angora's; she leads through enthusiasm and charm, and the island runs on the good time she keeps perpetually going. But the lightness should not be mistaken for softness. Hannah enjoys the new world's order as thoroughly as any giantess, and toward the toys who serve her island's leisure she carries the same casual, unhurried cruelty as the city she rules — the careless indifference of the comfortable, dressed up in sunshine and a smile.

She is, in the end, a giantess who rules by being irresistible. People come to Marisol for the party, and Hannah *is* the party — its brightest, biggest, most magnetic presence, holding a whole city together through nothing more complicated than the pull of her own boundless vitality.

## History

### Before TDay

Little has been established about Hannah's life before the world changed. Like all the transformed, she was once ordinary, before the Apotheosis Serum remade her into the towering kangaroo giantess she is now.

### After TDay

Hannah came into her own in the new world's culture of indulgence, where her boundless energy and magnetic presence made her a natural centre of gravity wherever revelry gathered. As Marisol grew into the new world's pleasure-capital — a tropical island given wholly to leisure — Hannah rose with it, becoming the undisputed life of its endless party long before she formally ruled it. In time the island was simply hers: not seized or schemed for, but settled upon the giantess who was already its beating heart.

She has ruled Marisol ever since as its Matriarch, keeping the party going and the island indulged, and holding it firmly within the new order. Like every Matriarch, she answers to no one above her save [Sin](sin).

## As Matriarch of Marisol

Hannah's rule is a lighter thing than her counterparts', but it is a Matriarch's rule all the same. She governs Marisol by being its magnetic centre — the presence the whole island orients toward — and by keeping its perpetual celebration running and its pleasures flowing. Beneath the sunshine, the city is hers: run for indulgence, kept within Sin's order, and held through the [power to command](matriarchs) that comes with being the undisputed heart of the place.

Her authority is absolute within Marisol and, like all Matriarchs, conditional on staying within the new world's order — which suits Hannah perfectly, since she wants nothing more than to keep her island exactly as it is. She shares with Alea's and Angora's Matriarchs the same easy mutual respect that holds between all the mega-cities; the dominant gender does not war with itself, and Hannah's energies point at keeping the party alive and the toys serving, never at any rival among the Matriarchs.

## Preferences

*The kinks and dynamics that define this character's encounters.*

Hannah's register is **playful, high-energy indulgence** — the domination of toys as one more pleasure of an island built for pleasure. She is exuberant and magnetic in her encounters as in everything, treating a toy as an amenity of the good time she's perpetually having: something to serve, entertain, and be used up in the warm, careless rhythm of Marisol's endless leisure. Her cruelty is unhurried and casual rather than sharp — the comfortable indifference of a giantess for whom a toy is simply part of the fun, savoured over a long sunlit afternoon and discarded as easily as a finished drink. Her great size only widens the gap between her and the small who serve her revelry.
