## Overview

Leona is a bunny-lion hybrid, [Sin's](sin) personal operative, and one of the very few individuals in the new world who can claim genuine, standing closeness to its architect. Her formal title is **bodyguard** — a holdover from the old world, when Sin was still a mortal researcher with mortal enemies and a great deal to hide. Sin needs no protecting now. But Leona kept the title, and the place at Sin's side that came with it, and turned the role into something more useful to a being who rules the world: she is the hand Sin sends out when she wants a thing done directly, quietly, and without delay.

Find a particular toy. Deliver a message to a particular person. Go somewhere, see to something, and report back. The tasks vary; what they have in common is that Sin would rather have them handled by someone she trusts than left to the apparatus. That someone is Leona, and her pride in it is bottomless.

## Appearance

Leona is a striking hybrid of bunny and lion, standing thirteen and a half feet — a shade taller, as it happens, than Sin herself, the sort of detail that suits a woman whose original job was to stand between her mistress and harm. The two halves of her heritage blend across her: the long ears and softer features of a rabbit set against the heavier build, the colouring, and the unmistakable mane-framed presence of a lion. The result is something more imposing than either parent stock alone, an apex frame with a predator's confidence in how it moves.

The serum left her endowed, as it does all the Transformed, in a feline form to match her lion's half. She is built powerfully — the body of someone who was, once, expected to use it — and she carries that power with the easy, lording assurance of a woman who has never doubted she is the largest and most dangerous thing in most rooms she enters.

## Personality

Leona's defining trait is pride — specifically, pride in her closeness to Sin. In a world where most never glimpse the First Matriarch and the toys taken to meet her are never seen again, Leona comes and goes from Sin's presence as a matter of course, trusted with errands Sin will hand to no one else. She knows exactly how rare that is, and she wears it openly. It is the centre of her sense of herself: not merely a giantess, not merely powerful, but *Sin's* — the one sent for when it matters.

That pride makes her formidable and a little vain, and intensely loyal in a way that is more proprietary than worshipful. She does not fawn over Sin so much as guard her unique standing with her, jealous of the access and quietly certain no one else has earned it. Toward everyone beneath that standing — which is to say nearly everyone — she is confident to the point of arrogance, accustomed to being obeyed and unbothered by the rare soul who hesitates. She is efficient, direct, and used to closing distance between Sin's wishes and the world's compliance by whatever means the errand requires.

## History

### Before TDay

Leona's role predates the new world. In the years when Sin was still a mortal genius building the foundations of SinCorp in secret — assembling the plans, the resources, and the network that TDay would one day require — she had the vulnerabilities of any mortal with dangerous ambitions and dangerous enemies, and Leona was her protection against them. As Sin's bodyguard in those years, Leona stood between her and the threats that came with what she was quietly building, in the dangerous interval before Sin became something no threat could touch.

### After TDay

TDay made Sin untouchable, and made Leona's original purpose obsolete in the same stroke. A being who reshaped the world and stands beyond any rival or assassin does not need a bodyguard. But the bond and the trust built in those vulnerable years did not evaporate with the danger that forged them, and Leona's place at Sin's side simply changed shape.

She became Sin's operative — a personal instrument for a Matriarch who finds the business of ruling tedious and would rather be in her laboratory than attending to the world's loose ends. Where the administrative apparatus of [Angora](angora) handles the ordinary running of things, Leona handles the particular: the errands Sin wants seen to directly, by a trusted hand, rather than filtered through layers of functionaries. She is dispatched to locate someone, to speak to someone, to retrieve or deliver or resolve, and to do it with the speed and discretion that come of being Sin's own. The title of bodyguard remained, an honorific from another era; the work became that of a right hand.

## As Sin's Operative

Leona's value to Sin is precisely that she is *not* the apparatus. SinCorp's branches and Angora's administration are vast, capable, and slow, and everything they touch leaves a trail through channels other people can see. Leona is a single trusted body Sin can point at a problem and expect resolved — off the books, off the org chart, answerable to no one but the First Matriarch herself.

The errands are varied and rarely explained. She may be sent to track down a specific toy Sin has reason to want; to carry a word to a person Sin would rather not contact through formal means; to look into something, or someone, and bring back what she finds. What unites the work is trust and immediacy: these are the things Sin wants handled now, and handled by someone whose loyalty she does not have to question. It is a position of real and unusual latitude, and Leona guards it as jealously as she once guarded Sin's person.

## Preferences

*The kinks and dynamics that define this character's encounters.*

Leona's signature is **sissification** and the lording of her enormous new body over the toys beneath her. The serum gave her a frame built to dominate, and she takes a specific, relishing pleasure in using it to unmake a toy's sense of himself — emasculating him, dismantling whatever pride he arrived with, and reshaping him into something soft and obedient under her. The power dynamic is the point: she savours the gulf between what she has become and what the small thing before her is, and she makes him feel every inch of it. A toy in Leona's hands is expected to know his place precisely, and to address her, pointedly, as **daddy** — the title encapsulating the whole of the dynamic she enjoys, her dominance and his emasculation folded into a single word she insists upon hearing.

## Usage Guide

*Notes for content creators working with this character.*

- Leona's **"bodyguard" title is a holdover**, not her current function. She was a real bodyguard before TDay, when Sin was mortal and vulnerable; now she is Sin's personal operative and fixer. Don't write her standing uselessly guarding an invulnerable Sin — write her *sent out* on Sin's errands.
- Her defining trait is **pride in her closeness to Sin.** She has access and trust almost no one else does, she knows it, and it is central to who she is.
- Her loyalty is **proprietary more than worshipful** — she guards her unique standing with Sin jealously, rather than fawning.
- Her kink is **sissification and dominance** — emasculating toys, lording her size over them, and demanding to be called **daddy.** This is her signature register.
- She is a hybrid: **bunny and lion.** Lean on the blend — the rabbit's features and ears against the lion's build and presence.
