## Overview

Eve is a bunny and a [Sylph](project-sylph) — and one of the more natural fits the program ever selected. Born Evan, he went by Eve long before [TDay](tday); a femboi to his core, he had always embraced his feminine side, and never saw the two as in tension. Like all males he was left small when the world changed, and might have ended up a [toy](toys) like the rest. Instead, when [Project Sylph](project-sylph) came into being, he proved an almost effortless candidate: a male whose femininity and appetite for domination were exactly the raw material the program was looking for.

Where some [Sylphs](project-sylph) carry their new station uneasily, Eve *revels* in his. Once, standing a few inches over an unsuspecting date was enough of a thrill for him. Now, at over eleven and a half feet, he has been handed a world's worth of that same delight — a towering twink turned loose on a setting that suits him perfectly. He remains, like all his class, beholden to [the Transformed](the-transformed) he answers to; but as an extension of a giantess's power, he has found a role he genuinely loves.

## Appearance

Eve is a striking bunny built on lithe, deliberately feminine lines. His fur is an unusual aqua blue, broken by soft creme across his face, chest, and hands, and he has long, floppy ears that hang all the way to the middle of his back. His hair is long and black, falling past his shoulders to his upper chest, and his eyes are a bright pink — the whole palette pitched, like everything about him, to be looked at.

He dresses the part with total commitment, in a look best described as *femboi punk*: a black skirt, thigh-high leggings under semi-transparent thigh-highs, an off-the-shoulder crop top, and matching arm sleeves. He finishes it with painted nails and claws, black lipstick, and a pink collar hung with an ankh pendant — the complete alt-hottie package, assembled with obvious care. He is, and clearly means to be, the most eye-catching thing in any room a [toy](toys) might share with him.

The [Sylph](project-sylph) process left him endowed in a humanoid form, dramatically outsized on his slender frame — a trait he carried, in proportion, even before his transformation, and one that sits at odds with the delicate figure he presents at first glance. At his full eleven-foot-seven he reads exactly as what he is: a Sylph's towering, middling scale, enough to loom over any toy, never enough to rival the giantesses he serves.

## Personality

Eve is playful, confident, and mischievous — an incorrigible tease who takes open, cheerful delight in his own presence. He is comfortable in his skin to the point of relish, and that ease is the engine of everything else about him. He does not brood, and he does not resent what he has become; the person who came through [Project Sylph](project-sylph) is thoroughly, gleefully at home in the new world.

The best shorthand for him is *giant brat* — literally. He carries himself with a bratty, taunting swagger, and he loves nothing more than lording his scale and status over the [toys](toys) beneath him, teasing and mocking them as he keeps them in their place. It is cruelty of a lighthearted, performative kind, cruelty as play rather than malice, and he is very good at it. Underneath the theatrics is a genuine, uncomplicated pleasure in domination — the same appetite he had in a smaller body, now given a stage exactly its size.

## History

### Before TDay

Even in the old world, Eve was already Eve. He embraced his femininity openly and made no apology for it — a femboi through and through, and one who understood exactly how disarming that could be.

He made something of a habit of catfishing men online, though not in the usual way. He never pretended to be someone he wasn't; he simply leaned into the assumption people made of him — the stereotypical soft, submissive femboi — and let them believe it. When a date finally happened, he would spring two surprises. The first was purely physical: he stood nearly six foot four, considerably taller than his pictures ever let on, and he enjoyed watching a man realize he was looking *up*. The second he saved for dates that went well — that the demure femboi they thought they'd met was, in fact, a demanding top, and a well-equipped one. The role reversal was the whole point. Taking men who fancied themselves big, tough "alphas" and turning the dynamic entirely on its head was, to Eve, simply the most fun a night could offer.

### After TDay

What became of Eve in the immediate aftermath of TDay is not known — like many, he vanishes from the record during the chaos of the early years. He resurfaces with the rise of [Project Sylph](project-sylph), for which he turned out to be an almost ideal candidate. His natural femininity checked the program's first requirement, and his obvious proclivity for domination suited the role perfectly; once SinCorp was satisfied that he had genuinely embraced the new world order, the rest was a formality.

The transformation gave him, at last, a scale to match his appetite. The few inches he once towered over his dates became a *few feet* and then several more — and Eve took to it with unabashed joy. Where the program had to work to reconcile some candidates to their new lives, it had no such trouble with Eve. He had, in a sense, been auditioning for the part his whole life.

## As a Sylph

As a [Sylph](project-sylph), Eve occupies the lieutenant's rung the class was built for — real authority over the [toys](toys), fully answerable to the [giantesses](the-transformed) above him. He wears it as a natural extension of his mistress's power, and he wields it with evident enjoyment: keeping toys in line is not a duty he tolerates but one he *savors*, an endless supply of small, powerless things to loom over, tease, and put in their place.

His standing is the ordinary Sylph's standing — genuine but conditional, towering over the toys and dwarfed by the women he serves — and that arrangement suits him completely. He has no ambition to rival [the Transformed](the-transformed); the pleasure, for Eve, was never in being the biggest thing in the world, only in being far, far bigger than whatever he had decided to torment that day.

## Preferences

*The kinks and dynamics that define this character's encounters.*

Eve is a bratty, exhibitionist top whose whole thing is role reversal — taking partners who read as big, dominant, or "tough" and flipping them beneath him, a thrill he once got from a few inches and now gets from towering feet. Toward toys he's a teasing, mocking size-dom, savoring the humiliation-play of looming over something tiny and powerless, all performance and swagger; his oversized humanoid endowment is central to how he asserts that, and the delight is in the theater of dominance as much as the act.

## Usage Guide

*Notes for content creators working with this character.*

- Eve is **male** (he/him) and goes by **Eve**, not Evan — he adopted the name well before TDay and has always embraced being a femboi. Don't write him as a woman or as reluctant about his femininity; it's central and entirely owned.
- He is a **contented, gleeful Sylph** who *revels* in his role — no resentment, no secret wound. Like all Sylphs he was selected in part for genuine buy-in (see [Project Sylph](project-sylph)); the new order fits him.
- His defining register is **"giant brat"** — playful, taunting, performative dominance. His cruelty toward toys is lighthearted and theatrical, not vicious.
- Core to him is **role reversal**: taking those who present as big or tough and putting them beneath him. Pre-TDay this was a party trick of a few inches; as a Sylph it's his whole scale.
- His **mistress is currently unnamed** — leave his specific patron open unless coordinating with canon, as his relationships may be filled in later.
- His data lists **humanoid** anatomy; he stands **11'7"**, mid-range for a Sylph. Avoid framing his height as a record — the world has no fixed ceilings.
- Keep any figures from his pre-TDay dating life **unnamed**; they aren't canon characters.
