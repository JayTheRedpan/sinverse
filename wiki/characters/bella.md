## Overview

Bella is a lioness and one half of an inseparable pair of twin sisters, the other being her sister Becka. Where most of [the transformed](the-transformed) hunt alone, Bella and Becka hunt together — and, more unusually, they *share*. The twins are defined by their bond: a giggling, bickering, fiercely close partnership that turns even their cruelty into a joint activity. To a [toy](toys), that closeness is no comfort. Two apex predators who finish each other's sentences and cooperate by instinct are far harder to survive than one.

Bella is the more excitable, performative half of the pair — quick to clap and squeal over a "fun" idea, prone to theatrics, and happy to let her sister be the one who thinks things through. She is no less dangerous for it.

## Appearance

Bella is a largely ordinary anthro lioness scaled up to roughly eighteen feet — tawny, athletic, and built with the easy muscle of a big cat. She has long brownish hair, which she wears in twin pigtails; this is, along with her sister's ponytail, one of the only reliable ways to tell the two of them apart, as they are otherwise identical. The pigtails suit her temperament: a little girlish, a little playful, a deliberate softness on a frame that is anything but.

The serum left her endowed in place of the genitalia it took — a humanoid length, identical to her twin's, which the two of them are equally happy to put to use on a shared toy.

## Personality

Bella is the bubbly, impulsive twin — animated, attention-seeking, and gleeful in a way that makes her cruelty feel almost casual. She narrates her fun out loud, gets visibly excited over her sister's suggestions, and treats the breaking of a toy as a game she and Becka are playing together. Underneath the giddiness is the same indifference to her victims that runs through all the transformed; she simply wraps it in playground enthusiasm, which somehow makes it worse.

Her defining relationship is with Becka. The twins squabble constantly — over toys, over whose idea was better, over nothing — but it never threatens their bond; bickering is just how they talk to each other, and it resolves the instant something more fun presents itself. Left to her own devices Bella might fight a sister tooth and claw for a prize, but the two of them long ago discovered they'd rather share than be apart, and that discovery is the engine of who they are.

## History

### Before TDay

Bella and Becka were the step-daughters of a wealthy human man — daughters of the lioness he'd married, and step-sisters to his human son, with whom they shared a home but no blood and no species. They grew up in money that wasn't theirs, looked down on by a step-brother who considered humans his betters and never let them forget it.

### TDay

When the transformations swept the world, the twins changed alongside every other woman — their family's wealthy estate collapsing around them as they outgrew it. In the chaos of that first day, their step-brother fled, abandoning them and driving off without looking back. The twins, newly enormous and newly hungry, were not the kind to simply let that go.

When they eventually ran their fugitive step-brother to ground at a remote family property, he became their **first toy** — and the experience cemented the dynamic that would define them. Rather than fight over him as two predators might be expected to, Bella and Becka discovered they enjoyed sharing far more, working in tandem on a single victim with the eerie coordination of twins. By the end they had stopped thinking of him as a brother at all, rechristened him as a pet, and kept him — the first of many toys to learn that being caught by one of the sisters meant being caught by both.

### After TDay

Bella and Becka remain inseparable, roaming the new world as a unit and keeping toys between them rather than apart. They have no faction and no cause beyond each other; their world is small, self-contained, and centered entirely on the two of them and whatever plaything currently holds their attention.

## Preferences

*The kinks and dynamics that define this character's encounters.*

Bella's defining kink — shared completely with her sister — is exactly that: **sharing a single toy**. The twins take a particular delight in using one victim together, often after squabbling over who gets to do what, and the act becomes a kind of collaborative performance between them. A toy caught by Bella and Becka is used from both ends at once, pinned between two coordinated predators who treat it as the shared centerpiece of their fun and barely seem to notice its suffering past how it serves their game. It is an experience few toys can physically endure — two apex giants, perfectly in sync, with none of the distraction or restraint that a single partner might occasionally show. The bickering and the sisterly one-upmanship only raise the intensity, each twin egging the other on. For Bella the appeal is as much about doing it *with Becka* as it is about the toy; the shared cruelty is a bonding ritual, and the plaything is mostly the thing they bond over.

## Usage Guide

*Notes for content creators working with this character.*

- Bella is almost always written **alongside Becka** — the twins are a unit, and their shared-toy dynamic is the whole point. A Bella scene without her sister should be the rare exception.
- She is the **bubbly, impulsive** half to Becka's slightly more deliberate one — keep the contrast light; they're variations on a theme, not opposites.
- Their **bickering is affectionate** and never a real threat to their bond — it's how they relate, and it resolves instantly.
- The two are physically **identical except for hair** — Bella's pigtails, Becka's ponytail.
- They have **no faction** — their allegiance is to each other.

## Did You Know

- Their step-brother was their **first toy** — he abandoned them on [TDay](tday), and they tracked him down and kept him as a pet.
- The twins **share** their toys rather than fight over them, a dynamic so intense that few playthings can survive it.
- Bella is the **more excitable** of the pair, prone to clapping and squealing over her sister's "adorable ideas."
- At roughly **eighteen feet**, the twins are middling in size among the transformed — but they come as a matched pair, which more than makes up for it.
