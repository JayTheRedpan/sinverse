What is Sinverse?

Sinverse is an adult anthro and human setting built around themes of size difference, futa supremacy, and extreme sexual domination. It's a shared creative universe of stories, art, and lore contributed by the community.

It is a world reshaped by one woman's discovery that transformed the world — leaving behind a landscape of towering predators and the tiny prey who live at their mercy.

New here? Start with Sinverse Rise — a short four-page comic told from the titular Sin's point of view, covering the discovery of her serum, its effects, TDay, and why she considers the world that followed a far better one.

How to Contribute

Sinverse is an open setting — a shared world that anyone is welcome to add to. Stories, art, characters, lore, interactive adventures: if you're drawn to the setting and want to make something for it, there's a place for it here.

You don't need to be a polished professional. The world grows through contributions big and small, and every creator is credited for their work. A single scene or sketch is a perfectly good way to begin.

A few principles keep the world consistent — chief among them that every character is an adult. The full guidelines walk through what you can contribute, how to stay consistent with canon, and how to get started.
